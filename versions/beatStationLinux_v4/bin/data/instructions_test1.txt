Please use SPACEBAR to tap to the beats. In addition to the beats, you can tap on the 'samas' (start of the tala cycle) using the key S. Please try to tap using both the keys. Use the PLAY button to play and then start tapping. Use the NEXT button to move on to the next song. Adjust the volume if needed. If you are not satisfied with your tapping, you can replay the excerpt and tap again. You can listen and tap to each excerpt atmost twice.
